**About the guest:**

[Dr. Amanda Cercas Curry](https://amandacurry.github.io/) is a researcher at CENTAI Institute, where she is working on applied NLP, fairness and evaluation. Her research interests lie at the intersection of Natural Language Processing (NLP), Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Feminism with the aim of building more just NLP systems. She is particularly interested in ethics, with a strong influence from feminism. Previously she was a postdoctoral researcher at MilaNLP in Bocconi University. She completed her PhD at Heriot-Watt University in Edinburgh. She also co-hosts a podcast where she discusses all things tech ethics, called Let's Chat Ethics.
