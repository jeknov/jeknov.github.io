**About the guest:**

[Simeng Sophia Han](https://sophiahan6.github.io/) is a final-year Ph.D. candidate at Yale University and Research Intern at Meta FAIR, USA. She focuses on LLM complex reasoning, neuro-symbolic reasoning and reasoning evaluation. Her recent work on the FOLIO benchmark has had a profound impact on improving language models' reasoning capabilities. Outside of research, she deeply enjoys mentoring students, organizing conferences and workshops, and contributing to a more inclusive computer science community. She is also a chair for Widening NLP from 2025 to 2027. Beyond academia, Simeng is currently engaged in foundational research on LLM reasoning at Meta GenAI frontier reasoning team. She has also previously worked on information retrieval at Google Deepmind and retrieval-augmented generation at AWS NLP group.
